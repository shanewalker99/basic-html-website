Images

This page is all about images. If you are looking for links or extra html follow the links.

JPG's

Jpg file format is good for photos, something with large color pallate with no transparent pixel.

GIF's

Gif is a good format for animated images, but lacks extensive color pallate and cannot have semi-opaque pixels.

PNG's

Png is a good format for logos, diagrams, etc that need transparent pixels. They aren't good for photos, as they take large amount of space for complex images.

SVG's

Svg is a format that is scaleable without losing definition, as it is a vector based image format. Infinitely scaleable.
